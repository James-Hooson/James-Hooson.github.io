Emotional Intelligence

What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional Intelligence (EQ) is the ability to understand emotions and fellings of yourself and those around you. It stives for a concious awareness of how you are felling or how others may feel.

How do I work on my Emotional Intelligence?

Working on your EQ can be a long process. Those who wish to improve their EQ usually start with daily meditations. This is not just sitting crosslegged, humming with your eyes closed. Its a state of being, where you bring yourself to the present. It helps alleviate us of our usual concerns, things from the past or things yet to come. These worries take us out of being present, in the now. To start improving your EQ, try and give yourself small windows of 5 or 10 minutes, and just listen and feel things around you, such as the sound of birds, he breeze on your skin, your chest rising and falling with each breath. This is the present.

What makes up Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional Intelligence can be boiled down to 5 key points;

  • Self-Awareness
  • Self-Regulation
  • Motivation
  • Empathy
  • Social Skills
