Te Whare Tapa Wha

Ta Mason Durie developed the Te Whare Tapa Wha model in 1984 to represent the Maori perspective on Health and Wellbeing. It is comprised of a house like structure, with Taha Tinana (physical), Taha Hinengaro (Mental and Emotional), Taha Wairua (spiritual), and Taha Whanau (Family wellbeing) being the walls and roof of the house. Our Whenua (Land) is the base of our structure.
